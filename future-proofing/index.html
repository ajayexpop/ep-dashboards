How these numbers are calculated

Each figure is dev-days invested - effort estimated from every story's Jira Task Size (XS = 0.5, S = 1.5, M = 4, L = 10, XL = 22.5, XXL = 35 dev-days) and summed across the stories in that lever. It measures effort directed at reducing cost, not money saved. A single story can advance more than one lever (for example an AI feature that also removes manual work), so the three totals overlap and are not meant to add up to 100%.
